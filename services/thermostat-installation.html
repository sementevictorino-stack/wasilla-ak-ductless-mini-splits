Smart thermostats offer advanced features that traditional thermostats cannot match including remote control via smartphone apps, learning algorithms that adapt to your schedule, energy usage reports and savings tracking, integration with smart home systems, and automatic adjustment based on weather forecasts. Many smart thermostats also qualify for utility rebates, helping offset the installation cost.
